How to Use Google Fonts :- 

--- 1) Go to https://fonts.google.com .
--- 2) Select any Font.
--- 3) Click on View Selected families.
--- 4) Paste link in "head" tag.
--- 5) Use font-family in Selected id/class .




Why Google Fonts are Used ?

--- 1) To reduce pressure on over-server by passing some pressure on Google's Server.
--- 2) To get More Fonts.
